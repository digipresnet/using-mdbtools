Acknowledging the Fontes database

  • Guidelines for citing from the CD-ROM version

For those citing their own entries or entries by others:

Doane, A. N., 'The Sources of Genesis A (Cameron A.1.1.1)', 1990, Fontes Anglo-Saxonici, CD-ROM Version 1.1, 2002

To find the publication date of the records on the CD-ROM database: Select the Anglo-Saxon text you are interested in; the date of publication will appear in the 'details' section below it. NOTE: individual records within a set may have been updated; if you wish to cite a particular record, go to the record you are interested in, where you will find both the date of publication and the date it was updated.

 

  • Guidelines for citing from the Web version

For those citing entries by others:

Doane, A. N., 'The Sources of Genesis A (Cameron A.1.1.1)', 1990, Fontes Anglo-Saxonici: World Wide Web Register, http://fontes.english.ox.ac.uk/, accessed July 2000

For contributors citing their own entries:

Doane, A. N., 'The Sources of Genesis A (Cameron A.1.1.1)', 1990, Fontes Anglo-Saxonici: World Wide Web Register, http://fontes.english.ox.ac.uk/

 

To find the publication date of the records on the Web: Select the Anglo-Saxon text you are interested in; under the heading 'titles' you will find the details for this text, where the date of publication is recorded as the 'date of records for the text'. NOTE: individual records within a set may have been updated; if you wish to cite a particular record, go to the 'source details' for the record, where you will find both the date of publication and the date it was updated.
